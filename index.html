ROBI Calculator

Calculate your ROBI score.

The ROBI Points method is a calculation that allows you to compare individual Total results achieved in different Junior / Senior bodyweight categories. The score is based on the individual's Total result, their bodyweight category - but not the lifter's exact bodyweight - and the actual World Record in their class.
